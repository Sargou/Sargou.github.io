Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The short version

Nosey is a free, editorial pet-insurance comparison site. The quiz runs entirely in your browser — your answers are not sent to us or stored by us. We don't require accounts, and we don't sell personal information.

What we collect

Nothing, by default. Quiz answers stay on your device and disappear when you leave the page.

Email (optional): if you choose to enter your email to receive your results, it's sent to our form provider solely to deliver that email and, if you opt in, occasional updates. Unsubscribe anytime with one click.

Analytics: if enabled, we use privacy-respecting, aggregate analytics (page views, button clicks) to improve the site. No profiles, no cross-site tracking by us.

Affiliate links

When you click "get a quote" links, you go to the insurer's website and we may earn a referral fee at no cost to you. Those links contain a tracking code so the insurer knows the visit came from us. What you do on the insurer's site — including any quote details you enter there — is governed by their privacy policy, not ours. We never see it.

Cookies & third parties

We don't set advertising cookies. Affiliate networks may set a cookie after you click an outbound link so referrals can be attributed (typically for 30–90 days). Embedded services (fonts, form provider, analytics) may process standard technical data like IP address per their own policies.

Your choices & contact

Don't want to share an email? Don't enter one — everything works without it. To have an email you previously submitted deleted, or for any privacy question, contact: hello@nosey.pet.

We may update this policy as the site evolves; the date above reflects the latest version.
